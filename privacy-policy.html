Introduction

Welcome to SkyzoneBD ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Make a purchase
  • Contact customer support
  • Participate in surveys or promotions

Personal information collected includes:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address
  • Business information (for B2B accounts)

Financial Information

We collect payment information through secure third-party payment processors. We do NOT store your credit card information on our servers.

Financial information collected:

  • Purchase history
  • Order details
  • Transaction records

Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (city level, for delivery purposes)
  • App usage data (pages viewed, features used)
  • Log data (access times, errors)

How We Use Your Information

1. Provide Services

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Manage your account
  • Provide customer support
  • Send order confirmations and updates

2. Improve Services

  • Analyze app usage and performance
  • Develop new features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Conduct research and analytics

3. Marketing (With Your Consent)

  • Send promotional offers and deals
  • Personalized product recommendations
  • Newsletter subscriptions (opt-in only)

4. Security & Legal

  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights and property

How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with:

Service Providers

  • Payment processors (secure transactions)
  • Delivery partners (shipping)
  • Cloud hosting services (data storage)
  • Analytics providers (app improvement)

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent illegal activities
  • Ensure user safety

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Encrypted Storage: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and updates
  • Secure Authentication: JWT tokens (no password storage)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Privacy Rights

Access & Update

You can access and update your personal information through:

  • Your account profile in the App
  • Contacting us at support@skyzonebd.com

Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information:

  1. Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account in the App
  2. Or email us at support@skyzonebd.com
  3. We will delete your data within 30 days

What happens after deletion:

  • Your account will be permanently closed
  • Order history will be anonymized for record-keeping
  • Personal information will be deleted from our active databases
  • Some information may be retained as required by law

Opt-Out

You can opt-out of:

  • Marketing emails (unsubscribe link in emails)
  • Push notifications (App settings)
  • Analytics tracking (device settings)

Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format by emailing support@skyzonebd.com

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at support@skyzonebd.com.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Retention periods:

  • Account information: While account is active + 30 days after deletion
  • Order history: 7 years (tax and legal requirements)
  • Support tickets: 3 years
  • Analytics data: 2 years (anonymized)
